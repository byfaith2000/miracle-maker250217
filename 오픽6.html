Home



I remember having a family gathering at home.
We invited some relatives over / for dinner.

We cooked some food for the gathering,
but we also ordered in some food from a delivery restaurant.

I helped set up the table before we ate.
The food tasted extra good because I was starving.

After the gathering, I helped clean up.
There were some leftovers, so I put them in the fridge.
I also cleaned the table and did the dishes.
And then, I started to clean my apartment.
I opened all the windows to refresh the air.
I also vacuumed and mopped the floors and dusted the furniture.
Plus, I took out the trash and the recycling.

Looking back, it was a very enjoyable gathering.
