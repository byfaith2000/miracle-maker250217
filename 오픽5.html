Home



I remember renovating my apartment a few years ago.
I redid the wallpaper and the windows.
I also redid the floors, the curtains and the blinds.
I also redid the bathroom, the kitchen and the living room.

After the renovation, my home looked brand new.
I was very happy with the new look.
It was worth the money I spent.

Plus, I remember getting some new furniture for my apartment.
I got a new sofa, a new tea table and some new dressers.
I also got a new bed, a new bookshelf and a new chair.
Sometimes, I rearranged the furniture for a change.

Also, I got some plants and pictures for the apartment. Plus, I got a new television and a new air-conditioner for the living room.
Next, I took a family picture and put it up on the wall.

So, these were the changes I remember.
