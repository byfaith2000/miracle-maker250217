I remember when the TV broke down at home.
It was very frustrating because I could NOT watch TV.
I could NOT fix the problem myself, so I called up a person to fix it.

Plus, I remember when the AC broke down at my place.
It was during the summer and it was very hot without air-conditioning.
Once again, I had to call up a person from the service center to fix it.

Next, I remember when one of the light bulbs in the bathroom went out.
So, I had to get a new bulb and replace the old one with the new one.

What else? I remember when the remote control for the TV did NOT work well.
So, I had to replace the batteries (베르뤼s) for the remote.

Also, I remember when water leaked from the water cooler.
There was water all over the place and it was a mess.
I had to wipe off the water from the floor.

Plus, I remember breaking a cup by accident.
So, these were the problems I remember having at home.
